Superstar

by. Mark Ezekiel P. Leonor

Superstar

The melody of words fills my ears.
As the harsh instruments turn my gears.
I look in the mirror, and it’s safe to say.
I want to be a metal artist someday.

To hear the crowd chant my band’s name.
To bang my head, and my mates do the same.
To do a solo in front of thousands on the guitar.
“Hey, Dad, I think I want to be a superstar”.
