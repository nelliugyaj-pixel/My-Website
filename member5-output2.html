The Constant Friend

by. Krisnel Faye M. Manlapaz

The Constant Friend


The world is built on shifting sand,
Where promises begin to fade,
But you remain a steady hand,
A light within the deepest shade.

They gave you sovereignty in name,
But power lies in how you stay;
Through every storm and every flame,
You never turn or walk away.

When shadows stretch and comforts tire,
And others leave the heavy task,
You are the hearth, the constant fire,
The only soul I have to ask.

For every "everytime" you stood,
A silent oath, a quiet grace,
You turned the bad into the good
by simply filling up the space.
