Buntod: The Place You’d Wanna Be

by. Mark Ezekiel P. Leonor

Buntod: The Place You’d Wanna Be


Have you ever been to Buntod Beach?
A true beach with a sand esplanade.
Locals there greet you with handshakes and nods.
A beautiful place that deserves plenty applauds.

Part of the Pearl of the Orient as we know it.
Travel by sea and you’ll know we’re not afraid to show it.
Look straight to see the marvelous scenes.
Look down to see all the beautiful fishes.

As the rays of sun hit your eyes.
As the sizzling pork begins to marbleize.
You feast your eyes once again on the shore.
You’ll be back here many more times, you’re sure.
