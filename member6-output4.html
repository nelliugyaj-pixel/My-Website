Unlike Any Other

by. Natalie Louise B. Busa

Unlike Any Other

Unlike any other, You were my safe zone. A place I go when I’m bothered, Yet somehow feel safe even when alone. You were the only one who knows me, Truly and emotionally. But sometimes when you’re not there, Sadness hits me deeply. You were still there when I was blue, So I thought you wouldn’t leave me. Through every storm I wandered through, Your quiet light would always see me. But seasons change and people too, And time can drift us far apart. Still every strength I have in me, Was first made brave inside your heart.
