Not Lost, Just Let Go

by Freia Cassandra T. Balin

Not Lost, Just Let Go

We were not perfect, we were flawed and true,
Yet every storm we faced, we faced as two.
We’d fall to anger, pride would rise above,
But patience pulled us back to gentle love.

You were my sunshine breaking through my gray,
My happy pill that chased my fears away.
I held you close when nights were cold and long,
We made each other steady, safe, and strong.

But dreams grew wings too powerful to stay,
Strict voices and bright futures called our way.
We loved so deep, yet still we understood,
To choose ourselves was choosing what we should.

So we let go with tears we couldn’t hide,
Still cheering for each other from the side.
No bitter end, no anger to defend,
Just love that changed, and chose to be our friend.
