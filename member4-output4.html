The Hercules Beetle

Noel Jayvee F. Francisco

The Hercules Beetle


Oh, how powerful you are
Fitting, named after a god
It always is riveting
To see you display most strength

For something so bulky yet small
Can endure uttermost weight
And maybe a man or two
Some thoughts I wonder from you

Is like any bug I met
Except with goliath strength
And diamond-like dermis
And adorned with dots of brown

It feasts on the decaying
And eats upon rotting sweets
Like bananas and cherries
And apples and oranges

The name ‘Hercules’ suits you
Too well with your strength and traits
Such a legendary name
For a little yet strong thing
