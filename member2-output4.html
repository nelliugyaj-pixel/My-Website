Whispers to the Sky

by Freia Cassandra T. Balin

Whispers to the Sky


Whispers to the Sky In silence deep,
I bow my head,
With thoughts unspoken,
fears I’ve said.
A gentle peace begins to flow,
As heavy burdens start to go.

Each whispered word,
a lifted weight,
A quiet trust in something great.
When storms arise and hopes feel small,
It’s prayer that catches every fall.

Through weary nights and blinding rain,
It calms my heart,
it soothes my pain.
Not every answer comes in sight,
But prayer still guides me with its light.

It’s more than words,
it’s where I rest,
A place where worries are confessed.
In prayer I find a sacred space,
A quiet touch of boundless grace.
