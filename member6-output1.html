GLEANED INSIGHTS

My first friend My first friend was named Lovely, A sweet petite girl that makes me happy. She makes me smile even when I’m sad, Our laughter would fill the room and the teacher would get mad. One day, I forgot my pen, She didn’t hesitate and lend me another pen. Although sometimes, my brain would get fuzzy. Everything I lose it, Lovely wouldn’t get mad. I feel guilty for losing it, so I bought her a new one so she won’t get sad. She’s my first and my best friend, Thanks to her, my childhood was very happy. Without her, my childhood won’t be that happy, But thanks to her, me myself won’t always be grumpy
