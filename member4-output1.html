MY FRIEND ADAM

by. Noel Jayvee F. Francisco

MY FRIEND ADAM

You were my strength and resilience
You were my hero that I follow
Day after day I always keep close to you
And you’ve always showed me what’s best

However, this day is my worst
I hated myself because I wasn’t there
T couldn’t handle seeing you lie down motionless
On the hospital bed

My thoughts on my actions
It all fogged up my mind
I cried and wept, day and night
As I sulk in my bed my throat sore from the weeping

The picture frame with us
Now filled with longing and grief
Sits there quietly as the moonlight highlights it
It’s glass sparkling on your side

I remembered something from you
A saying so memorable
“If I’m gone, I want you to remember our best memories with each other.”

It was your last command for me
And so I played those memories, over and over
Until I teared up tears of joy and nostalgia
And softly smiled with gratitude

The picture frame sparkles again
I stared at it, remembering those little things we shared
And I kept that picture frame as a way to remember you
I love you, Adam
Rest in peace, my dear friend
