GLEANED INSIGHTS


Felix, the smart and creative student
Strong-willed and passionate
He writes stories in entertaining ways
And isn’t afraid to explore new topics

He holds his pen with authority and comfort
And motivates people young or old
He’s praised and kind
A boy with lots of comfort, and stories to be told

No matter how tough the situation is
He’ll keep finding new ways
With joy he strokes his pen
And is composed without pensions
