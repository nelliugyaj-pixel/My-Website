Noel Jayvee F. Francisco

Lechon Manok MEAL


Everyone eventually hears this question
About what is the greatest food to them, only them
For I, who is a foodie, love eating savory
Specifically, chicken that is coal-grilled and glazed

My preference for sauce is savory with a kick
And the color of skin is charred and chocolate-brown
That is crispy or delicate, either way works well
And the meat being tender, juicy, and flavorful

I like to pair the chicken with rice to balance taste
And to make the meal more filling and energizing
I like to serve sauce in a small dish or by the rice
And I will use the bone to hold the meat to my mouth

To make it a true ‘meal’, it needs to have beverage
Iced tea, pineapple juice, or sprite works wonders for me
I will then sit on my seat and feast like a glutton
But no meal is whole with it, and it is family
