The Silent Bridge

by. Krisnel Faye M. Manlapaz

The Silent Bridge


It isn't a shout or a loud, ringing bell,
But a secret sanctuary where the weary can dwell.
Its role is the anchor in the middle of the storm,
Keeping the heart steady and the spirit kept warm.

The importance of prayer is the peace it imparts,
A bridge over canyons that divide human hearts.
It carries our burdens from the dark to the light,
Providing the vision when we lose our own sight.

When the world is too heavy and the road is too long,
This bridge is the melody of a quiet, deep song.
It connects where we are to the grace we still need,
A constant companion in every word, every deed.
