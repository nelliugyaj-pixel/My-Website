The memories we made

by. Zophia A. Rivera

The memories we made

We built our world from jokes and dreams, From midnight talks and random songs, From messy thoughts and wild schemes Where both of us somehow belong. You know my laugh before it starts, The stories written on my face, The map of all my fragile parts I never show the public space. If storms arrive, we’ll stand our ground, If roads divide, we’ll find our way, For friendship’s not in being bound-- It’s choosing still to walk and stay.
